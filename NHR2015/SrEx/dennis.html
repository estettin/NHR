Dennis Regan — Senior Vice President/Research

Dennis joined Norman Hecht Research in November, 1991. His primary responsibility is to work with our television clients with regard to ratings and sample measurement methods. This includes taking the lead in investigating ratings anomolies and discrepancies and determining what can be done to remedy the situation or educate the client on how to use the data to improve its ratings performance.

Before joining Norman Hecht Research, Dennis was Director, Program Planning Research at HBO. His research played a key role in shaping HBO's original movies and stand-up comedy strategies. Dennis' team was also pivotal in the development of the successful series Not Necessarily The News, 1st & Ten, Tales from the Crypt and Dream On. He also introduced and institutionalized the use of network television promotion testing techniques.

Prior to that, Dennis was Assistant Research Director, CBS Television Stations. He had overall responsibility for the station group's programming research and helped develop a news talent tracking study, and upgraded the department's sales research support. Prior to CBS, Dennis' research experience also includes working for an advertising agency, a TV sales rep, and the ABC Television Network. Dennis has a Master's of Business Administration degree from Adelphi University.
