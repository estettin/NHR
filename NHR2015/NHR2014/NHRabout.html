Who We Are and What We Do

Norman Hecht Research Inc., based on Long Island, is one of the leading market research and consulting companies in the United States specializing in the media industry.

Some of the leading media companies have turned to us to help them better understand their business opportunities, solve their business problems and assist in building strategies for improving performance.

We provide appropriate, effective, practical and sound research to our clients so they can feel confident in the recommendations offered.

We provide you with the information you need to make informed business decisions.
